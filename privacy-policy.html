Last updated: July 2026

01

Information We Collect

We collect information in two ways: information you give us directly, and information collected automatically.

Information you provide. When you submit a form on the Site, such as booking a strategy session or requesting a live demo, we may collect your name, email address, phone number, company name, website URL, monthly ad spend, and any message or goals you share with us.

Information collected automatically. Like most websites, we use analytics and advertising tools that automatically collect data such as IP address, browser type, device information, pages visited, and referring URLs. This includes cookies and pixels from Google (including Google Analytics and Google Ads) and Meta (Facebook and Instagram Ads Pixel).

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Comply with legal obligations and protect against fraud or misuse

How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information.
